Discoveries

  1. PM 2.5 is often in the unhealthy range in many places in china, however most of the other particles are in the good range.
  2. PM 2.5 levels in Saint Paul for the last 30 days have rarely even reached the moderate range.
  3. Both PM 2.5 and PM 10 levels reached the unhealthy for sensitive groups range multiple times in the last 30 days.
  4. In the last 30 days the air quality in Mexico City has been about the same as in Beijing.
  5. Delhi, India is one of the most polluted cities in the world. In the past 30 days it has consistently been in the unhealthy range and has reached the hazardous range multiple times.
  6. Air quality in Duluth, MN has rarely been worse than "good" over the past 30 days.
